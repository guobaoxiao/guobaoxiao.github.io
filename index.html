Guobao Xiao  肖国宝

    Professor

    Tongji University, Shanghai, China

    x-gb(at)163.com

He is a Professor at Tongji University (同济大学).

He is IEEE Senior Member, and he received the Ph.D. degree from Xiamen University in 2016.

From 2016 to 2018, he was a postdoctor. His research interest includes Computer Vision, Feature Matching and Image Fusion.
